Our Services

Comprehensive orthotic and prosthetic solutions to meet your unique needs

What We Offer

At Saahir Jooma Medical Orthotist & Prosthetist, we provide a wide range of services to help you achieve optimal function and comfort. Our team of experts works closely with you to develop personalized solutions that address your specific needs and goals.

Orthotics

Orthotics are custom-designed devices that support, align, prevent, or correct deformities and improve the function of movable parts of the body. Our orthotic services include:

  • Custom Foot Orthotics: Designed to address foot pain, plantar fasciitis, and other conditions affecting the feet.
  • Ankle-Foot Orthoses (AFOs): Support for weak ankle muscles and improve gait for patients with neurological conditions.
  • Knee Orthoses: Provide stability and support for knee injuries and conditions.
  • Knee-Ankle-Foot Orthoses (KAFOs): Comprehensive support for patients with weakness or instability affecting multiple joints in the lower limb.
  • Hip-Knee-Ankle-Foot Orthoses (HKAFOs): Full lower extremity support for patients with significant neuromuscular conditions or spinal cord injuries.
  • Hip Orthoses: Support for hip dysplasia, post-surgical recovery, and other hip conditions that require stabilization or positioning.
  • Spinal Orthoses: Support for spinal conditions such as scoliosis, kyphosis, and post-operative recovery.
  • Upper Extremity Orthoses: Support for wrist, elbow, and shoulder conditions.
  • Cranial Orthoses: Custom helmets for infants with plagiocephaly (flat head syndrome) and other cranial conditions requiring reshaping.
  • Compression Stockings: Medical-grade compression for circulation issues, edema, and post-surgical recovery.
  • Assistive Devices: Professional fitting and customization of canes, crutches, and walkers to optimize mobility and safety.
  • Wheelchairs: Custom wheelchair fittings and modifications to meet individual mobility needs and lifestyle requirements.

Prosthetics

Our prosthetic services focus on creating comfortable, functional artificial limbs that help restore mobility and independence after limb loss. We offer:

  • Upper Extremity Prosthetics: From partial hand to shoulder disarticulation prostheses, we provide a range of options from cosmetic to highly functional devices.
  • Lower Extremity Prosthetics: Custom-designed prosthetic legs for all levels of amputation, from partial foot to hip disarticulation.
  • Microprocessor Prosthetics: Advanced technology that adapts to your movement patterns and environmental conditions.
  • Sports and Activity-Specific Prosthetics: Specialized prostheses for running, swimming, and other recreational activities.
  • Pediatric Prosthetics: Growing with your child from infancy through adolescence.

Rehabilitation Support

Our rehabilitation support services help you adapt to your new orthotic or prosthetic device and maximize its benefits. Our comprehensive approach includes:

  • Gait Training: Learn to walk efficiently and confidently with your new prosthesis or orthosis.
  • Balance and Coordination Exercises: Improve stability and prevent falls.
  • Strength Training: Build the muscles needed to use your device effectively.
  • Activity-Specific Training: Practice daily activities that are important to you.
  • Ongoing Support: Regular follow-up appointments to ensure your device continues to meet your needs.

Pediatric Services

We provide specialized care for children with orthotic and prosthetic needs. Our pediatric services include:

  • Growth-Accommodating Designs: Devices that can be adjusted as your child grows.
  • Child-Friendly Materials: Lightweight, durable materials that are comfortable for active children.
  • Colorful and Customizable Options: Let your child express their personality through their device.
  • Family Education: We teach parents and caregivers how to help their child use and care for their device.
  • School Coordination: We work with teachers and school staff to ensure your child's needs are met in the educational setting.

Home Visit Services

We understand that mobility challenges can make it difficult for some patients to visit our practice. That's why we proudly offer home visit services to ensure everyone has access to quality orthotic and prosthetic care.

  • Comprehensive Care: We bring our expertise directly to your home, providing the same high-quality assessment, fitting, and follow-up care as in our practice.
  • Hospital Visits: We also provide services directly to patients in hospitals, working closely with your healthcare team.
  • Convenience: Ideal for patients with limited mobility, transportation challenges, or complex medical needs.
  • Personalized Environment: Assessments in your daily living environment help us better understand your specific mobility challenges.
  • Family Involvement: Home visits allow family members and caregivers to be actively involved in the care process.

To schedule a home visit or learn more about this service, please contact our office.

Custom Fabrication

We partner with trusted external fabrication specialists to ensure each device is crafted with precision and care. By working with experienced external labs, we're able to deliver high-quality solutions tailored to your unique needs. Our fabrication services include:

  • 3D Scanning: Accurate digital measurements of your body to ensure a perfect fit.
  • CAD/CAM Technology: Computer-aided design and manufacturing for precise device creation.
  • Adjustments and Modifications: Tailored refinements based on your feedback to optimize comfort and function.
  • Repairs and Maintenance: Coordinated with our fabrication partners to keep your device in top condition.
  • Material Options: A wide selection of materials to match your lifestyle and clinical requirements.
