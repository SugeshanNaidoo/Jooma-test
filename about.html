Learn about our history, mission, and the dedicated team behind our services

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Mr.Jooma, our practice began with a simple mission,which is to provide exceptional orthotic and prosthetic care that empowers individuals to live their lives to the fullest.

Though we are at the beginning of our journey, our vision is rooted in the same values that define excellence in this field, which is, personalized care, innovative solutions, and a deep commitment to every patient we serve.

As an emerging provider, we strive to build a reputation for clinical expertise, compassionate service, and the integration of cutting-edge technology into every aspect of our care.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life for our patients by providing exceptional orthotic and prosthetic care that maximizes function, comfort, and independence.

Our Vision

To be the preferred provider of orthotic and prosthetic services, recognized for clinical excellence, innovation, and patient-centered care.

Core Values

  • Excellence in patient care
  • Integrity and professionalism
  • Innovation and continuous improvement
  • Compassion and empathy
  • Teamwork and collaboration

Our Certifications & Affiliations

Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA)

Our practitioners are registered with the HPCSA under the Professional Board for Orthotics and Prosthetics, ensuring adherence to nationally regulated standards of care and ethical practice.

South African Orthotic and Prosthetic Association (SAOPA)

We are proud members of SAOPA, which promotes professional development, advocacy, and the advancement of orthotic and prosthetic care in South Africa.

International Society for Prosthetics and Orthotics (ISPO)

As part of the global prosthetics and orthotics community, we maintain involvement with ISPO to contribute to international best practices and stay aligned with evolving global standards.
